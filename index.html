Who We Are

Conext Asia, founded in Vietnam in 2022, is a technology hub that connects green and sustainable innovations with local and global markets across Asia-Pacific. We serve as a bridge between global expertise and regional opportunities, empowering businesses and communities to embrace sustainable growth.

Mission:

To accelerate the adoption of sustainable technologies in Vietnam and Southeast Asia by building strong partnerships and fostering real-world applications.

Vision:

By 2030, to become Asia-Pacific's leading AIoT-driven hub for environmental and sustainability solutions.
